# Rhize Creative — Service Packages (Internal)

⚠️ NOT public-facing. This is our internal pricing framework. We quote per project.

---

## Tier 1: Concept Book
**For:** Clients who want the vision but will manage their own build.

**Includes:**
- Site visit + consultation
- Design concept development
- Concept book (PDF): mood boards, spatial layout, plant palette, material selections
- Plant palette with species, sizes, and placement
- One round of revisions

**Typical Range:** $3,000 – $8,000
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks

**Ideal client:** Has a contractor already. Wants design direction. Smaller properties or focused areas.

---

## Tier 2: Full Design + Documentation
**For:** Clients who want construction-ready plans.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Detailed planting plan with quantities and spacing
- Hardscape layout with materials spec
- Grading and drainage recommendations
- Irrigation zones (schematic, not engineering)
- Construction documentation package
- Two rounds of revisions

**Typical Range:** $8,000 – $25,000
**Timeline:** 6-8 weeks

**Ideal client:** Has a contractor or will hire one. Wants a complete package to hand off. Mid-to-large properties.

---

## Tier 3: Full Service (Design + Construction Management)
**For:** Clients who want one person from concept to completion.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Contractor sourcing and bid management
- Project management through construction
- On-site oversight (weekly or as-needed)
- Plant installation management
- Post-install walkthrough and punch list
- 60-day check-in after completion

**Typical Range:** $25,000 – $75,000+ (design fee — construction costs separate)
**Timeline:** 3-6 months depending on scope

**Ideal client:** High-end residential. Wants it done right without managing contractors themselves. Values quality over speed.
